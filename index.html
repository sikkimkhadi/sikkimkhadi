    • Welcome to Sikkim Khadi and Village Industries Board
    Sikkim Khadi & Village Industries Board (SKVIB) is an autonomous body of Government of Sikkim under the Commerce & Industries Department. While it was established in 1963, the Sikkim Khadi & Village Industries Board Act of 1996 covers its functions and objectives which are as follows :
    1. Social objective of generating employment to the rural artisans in the field of khadi and village industries
    2. The economic objective of producing saleable Khadi & village industries goods
    3. The wider objective of creating self reliance amongst the people and building up of a strong rural community spirit
    4. Implementation of government schemes targeting rural employment generation
    5. Impart training and support in establishment of khadi institutions, societies and village industries units.
